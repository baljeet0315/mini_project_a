About the job
Step into a pivotal role shaping the future of AI-driven testing at enterprise scale. As an AI/GenAI Test Lead, you will configure intelligent Test Case Agents, drive platform adoption, and ensure AI-generated outputs meet the highest quality standards across global customer teams.
Join EPAM to engineer solutions that matter. From AI to cloud transformation, you'll collaborate with top-tier innovators, gain autonomy to explore your ideas, and grow your skills in a culture built for tech excellence.
 
Responsibilities

Collaborate with Test Leads and QA teams to understand testing workflows and translate requirements into effective agent configurations
Configure and create Test Case Agents on an enterprise Agentic AI platform tailored to individual team needs and objectives
Validate AI-generated test cases and outputs to ensure quality, accuracy, and alignment with project standards
Lead onboarding, adoption, and post-adoption activities, providing hands-on support to customer teams integrating AI testing capabilities
Develop process documentation, user guides, and knowledge articles to enable seamless platform adoption and team self-sufficiency
Coordinate with scrum, platform, and support teams to resolve issues, drive enhancements, and represent progress in client-facing discussions
 
Requirements

Proven experience using Selenium with Java for test automation across enterprise-scale QA environments
Strong QA expertise with hands-on experience in Agile/Scrum methodologies and end-to-end testing workflows
Deep understanding of SDLC/STLC principles with proven experience in test case creation and validation
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills, including the ability to train teams on new tools and lead client-facing demos
Strong analytical and cross-functional coordination skills to manage issue resolution and platform improvements
Proficiency with Jira, Confluence, and test management platforms, with exposure to AI, GenAI, or Agentic AI platforms and enterprise adoption activities
