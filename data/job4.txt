
The Position 

The Chief Architect, Geointelligence leads the Chief Architect Office that is responsible for developing and executing a comprehensive technology roadmap to drive business growth, analyzing market needs, and overseeing R&D to deliver strategic long-term plans for earth and space observation platforms, data and services. The role includes leading the Technology Strategy group and IP Management function with close collaboration with the New Business team to support business capture, assess markets, and enhance product competitiveness. This role requires a unique blend of technical expertise, business acumen, and leadership skills to drive innovation, collaboration, and growth across the organization.

Key Responsibilities

In cooperation with Business Development, analyze market needs, trends, and competitor activity to identify opportunities for growth and innovation
Develop and execute a comprehensive product and technology roadmap that aligns with the company's overall business strategy and goals
Oversees Product Management who builds out the Chief Architect’s vision for product roadmap and specifications
Develop and implement IP strategy, including establishing technology and competitive landscapes as well as filing strategies.
Manage business area IP portfolio from invention creation, IP Steering Committee throught to patent filing, prosecution and maintenance, ensuring the protection of current and future technology development
Provide guidance on non-standard IP terms for customer contracts and IP rights assertion issues with customers and competitors.
Manage development, security, and operations (DevSecOps) for software development lifecycles throughout business area programs and products
Participate in capture and proposal phases to develop conceptual solutions and assess their feasibility
Lead the development of deliverable system technical baselines for bids, focusing on maximizing common elements and minimizing custom development
Provide direction to Product Management to decompose deliverable system-level capabilities into common element performance requirements and technical baseline, and configuration control common element technical baselines
Provide direction to Product Management to collect, schedule, establish funding for, and insert product improvements ahead of customer need
Oversee R&D efforts to ensure the development of innovative, high-quality products that meet customer needs and stay ahead of the competition
Oversee the creation of business cases for new common element early research and development to TRL6, including make/buy decisions
Hand off the as-bid technical baseline to newly awarded program team, providing introductions to customers and ensuring a smooth transition
Collaborate with Customer Program/Product Managers to ensure alignment with customer needs and expectations
Acts as main internal customer for MDA Products., with decision rights over whether a new variant of a product is acceptable
Supports the Program Manager in managing customer expectations and negotiating mutually beneficial solutions that meet product requirements and customer needs
Assists the Program Manager in maintaining product integrity by ensuring all changes are formally approved by the Product Manager and Chief Architect office, guaranteeing that only authorized modifications are made to our products
Offer technical guidance and mentorship to design teams, especially product development teams
Foster open communication and collaboration among teams
Establish a clear line of technical authority
Provide exposure to senior management to build credibility and visibility for team members
Develop and maintain technical expertise in program-specific areas

Leadership And Management

Manage all employees within the function, including planning, staffing, budgeting, managing expense priorities, and recommending and implementing changes to methods
Create new technology baselines aligned with MDA Space corporate policies, ensuring that the business area is always operating in a compliant and responsible manner
Assess external environmental factors that impact the organization, using independent judgment and risk analysis to make decisions that minimize risk and maximize opportunity
Define and implement a long-term plan for the key roadmap strategy to expand the business area, identifying new opportunities and optimizing existing ones
Provide direction on policy to managers and contributors or department leads, ensuring that all employees understand the company's vision, mission, and objectives

Market Awareness: Staying informed about industry trends, competitor activity, and emerging technologies, to identify opportunities and inform business decisions, using sources such as industry reports, conferences, and social media
